ALTA 2026
We are the leading annual conference in African languages, literature, and cultural studies. Come join other African language stakeholders in this annual event.

The conference on teaching and learning African languages

The ALTA Annual Conference is where the leaders and stakeholders of African languages come together to explore groundbreaking approaches in language pedagogy, cultural integration, and community engagement.
Conference Theme: African Languages Across Disciplines, Professions, and Communities
